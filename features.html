  • Animation Process


  • 1. Researching

    First off, we learn as much as possible about your brand and your product and service.
    How? We send clients a brief with questions about their brands values, identity, and target audience.
    We also ask about the videos goals and the tone it should have.


  • 2. Script: Defining the Concept

    What’s the message you wish the video to transmit?
    That’s what the second animation step is all about: defining the core message of your piece and writing a cohesive script around it.
    No matter which concept we are working on, we develop a simple and understandable script that lets your message shine through.


  • 3. Storyboard

    The third step of the animation production process is crafting the storyboard.
    Put simply, that’s like a visual version of the script.
    Following a comic book-like format, we illustrate the keyframes of your future video.
    We also add a caption of the script under each image to use as a reference.


  • 4. Deciding the Visual Style

    These are illustrations that portray the video’s aesthetic.
    They include the art design of every important element that appears on screen:
    the characters, product, color palette, typography, and the keyframes of the video.


  • 5. Illustration and Animation

    The moment we were all waiting for!
    Once we got your approval on all the previous animation phases, our skilled animation artist set out to bring the illustrations to life.
    Using sophisticated software, they give movement to the characters and other elements that appear on the screen.
